Here is a day‑by‑day list of notable global events from December 1 to December 7, 2024, roughly ordered within each date by importance and impact.
December 1, 2024

    World AIDS Day observed worldwide, focusing on HIV/AIDS prevention, treatment access, and stigma reduction.

    Continuing high‑intensity air and ground operations in the Gaza conflict, with explosive‑weapons contributing to a large number of civilian casualties that month.

December 2, 2024

    International Day for the Abolition of Slavery highlighted modern slavery, trafficking, and forced‑labor issues at the UN and in many countries.

    World Computer Literacy Day promoted digital inclusion and basic computer‑skills outreach, especially in underserved regions.

December 3, 2024

    International Day of Persons with Disabilities raised global awareness of disability rights, accessibility, and inclusion.

    Ongoing rebel advances in Syria continued to reshape the military landscape in the south and central regions.

December 4, 2024

    Indian Navy Day commemorated the Indian Navy’s role in national defense and maritime security.

    Wildlife Conservation Day focused on protecting endangered species and combating habitat loss worldwide.

    French political tensions intensified as Prime Minister Michel Barnier faced a no‑confidence challenge in parliament, contributing to a broader government‑crisis narrative.

December 5, 2024

    International Volunteer Day celebrated unpaid community service and civic engagement around the world.

    World Soil Day underscored the importance of healthy soil for food security, climate resilience, and sustainable agriculture.

    In Syria, rebel forces continued pushing eastward, with reports of fleeing Syrian soldiers crossing into Iraq as the Deir ez‑Zor‑area offensive unfolded.

December 6, 2024

    Romania’s presidential‑election annulment by the Constitutional Court after allegations of significant foreign (Russian‑linked) interference became one of the week’s most important political headlines.

    Rebel holdings in Syria expanded further, including capture of Homs‑city‑area sites and movement toward Palmyra.

December 7, 2024

    International Civil Aviation Day emphasized innovation and safety in global air transport.

    In India, Armed Forces Flag Day honored military personnel and raised funds for welfare schemes.

    Reports for the month of December 2024 showed that explosive‑weapon attacks—especially in Gaza—caused thousands of civilian deaths and injuries, underscoring the scale of suffering in ongoing conflicts.
